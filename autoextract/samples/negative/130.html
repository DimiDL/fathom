Registrar

Welcome to the Office of the Registrar. We serve HMS students, faculty, and alumni by maintaining student records, processing course registration, providing documentation to support application and licensure processes, and maintaining the HMS Course Catalog. We serve non-HMS medical students by coordinating the HMS Exchange Clerkship Program, which allows final-year students from other medical schools to participate in HMS clinical electives. Please visit our pages below for detailed information and resources.
