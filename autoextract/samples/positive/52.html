New Releases The New Releases page lists all of the current week's new comics, graphic novels, and trade paperbacks. Missed last week? We also provide convenient links to the new release lists for each of the past 8 weeks.
New In Stock The New In Stock page is all about back issues. The New In Stock page provides a list of all the back issues that have recently been added to our inventory, updated in real time as items are posted. New consignments and items assigned to upcoming auctions are also listed here. We add thousands of back issues each week. If you want to keep up with all the awesome comics we get in stock, all you have to do is check back with the New In Stock page.
Comic Auctions Our monthly comic book auction contains comics from the Golden Age, Silver Age, Bronze Age, and Modern Age of comics, with values ranging from about $20 to hundreds and thousands of dollars.
CGC graded and CBCS graded Comics We carry a large selection of comics that have been professionally graded and encapsulated by CGC or CBCS. Also be sure to check out our Slabbed Comic Storage Boxes, a great way to help protect your CGC and CBCS slabs during storage and shipping.
Comic Value Packs Our Comic Value Packs are a fun, affordable way to get a lot of comics without spending much. Each value pack is grouped around a theme and provides 2 lbs. of comics (approximately 18-22 comics)! See the Comic Value Packs page for a list of available packs and prices.
Coverless and Incomplete Golden Age Comics Incomplete comics include coverless comics, single pages that have been encapsulated by a grading service (typically only done with high value Golden Age comics), and comics that are missing one or more pages. Incomplete comics can be a more affordable way to own a part of a comic that would otherwise be too expensive. Some collectors who own an incomplete book may be on the hunt for the missing piece that would make that book complete, such as a missing centerfold or cover.
Pulp magazines We buy and sell all pulp magazines.
Comic Supplies A selection of bags, boards, and other supplies for storing and protecting your collection.
Gift Certificate Looking for a gift but not sure what issues to get for the comic collector in your life? A mycomicshop gift certificate is always a good option.
