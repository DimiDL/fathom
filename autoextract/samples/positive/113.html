Product Details

Item Number Item No. 2725059161970P
Let your little hero swing into action with this cool Spider-Man Backpack. The design features a full-sized, hard-padded Spidey mask and plenty of room inside for all the latest crime-fighting accessories, including a tech sleeve for a tablet or laptop.

Safety

⚠ WARNING: CHOKING HAZARD - Small Parts. Not for children under 3 years.

⚠ CAUTION Entanglement hazard. Keep away from very young children. Do not place around neck.

Magic in the details

  • Personalize up to 10 characters, including spaces
  • Hard-padded Spider-Man mask design with eye appliqués
  • Rubber ''MARVEL'' appliqué
  • Double-zip main compartment
  • Front, full-size zip pocket
  • Two mesh exterior side pockets
  • Padded, adjustable shoulder straps
  • Top carry handle
  • Interior tech sleeve for tablets and laptops
  • Interior ID label
  • Coordinates with our Spider-Man Lunch Box, sold separately

The bare necessities

  • Ages 3+
  • All man-made materials
  • 16'' H x 12'' W x 5 1/2'' D
  • Imported

Shipping & Delivery

Most items ordered via Standard Delivery will arrive within 4-5 business days, personalized items within 6-7 business days. Exceptions include items shipped from locations other than our main warehouse. Express Delivery is available for an extra charge.

I ordered this backpack for my 5 year old son. One picture shows it on my son’s back for size. I wish Disney store would show models with the backpacks on for size. I love how well made this backpack is! The Marvel logo had protective plastic on it when I received it. It has 2 zipper parts. The main part has a sleeve for a laptop. It expands well. In the pictures, I showed the inside with a binder, folder, notebook and pencil case just to show the size. The front zipper pockets is just open, no extra pockets or places to put pencils, pens etc. I chose to have my son’s name put on the strap. I’ve very pleased with how it turned out. I would definitely recommend this backpack for ANY age!
