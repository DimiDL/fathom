  • Inspired by the original 1978 Kenner Star Wars 3 3/4-inch action figures!
  • Packaged on classic 6-inch by 9-inch cardbacks with faux aging.
  • Each figure has 6 points of articulation and an accessory.
Go old school with the Star Wars The Retro Collection Action Figures Wave 1 Case! 6 of the original 12 Star Wars action figures from 1978 are back almost exactly like you remember them. Luke and Darth Vader retain their retractable telescoping lightsabers. Han, Leia, Chewbacca, and the Stormtrooper include their blaster pistols. And for the most discriminating of fans, each figure is just a smidgen different in size and detail that with a little study, you'll be able to tell them from the original releases. Ages 4 and up.

Case includes 6 individually packaged action figures:
1x Darth Vader
1x Luke Skywalker
1x Stormtrooper
1x Princess Leia Organa
1x Chewbacca
1x Han Solo
